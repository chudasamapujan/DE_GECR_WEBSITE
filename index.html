About GEC Rajkot

Our History

Established in 2004, Government Engineering College, Rajkot has been a pioneer in technical education in Gujarat. We have consistently maintained high academic standards and produced skilled engineers who contribute to the nation's development.

Vision & Mission

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, producing competent engineers with strong ethical values and leadership qualities, contributing to the technological advancement of society.
